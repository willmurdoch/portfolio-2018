Seamless, lightweight, and trusted, DuPont™ Intexar™ Health is innovative
textile technology that delivers advanced wearable health care.

The Intexar Solution.

DuPont™ Intexar™ is a revolutionary ink and film that integrates seamlessly into textiles. It transforms materials into smart wearables and provides an effective way to sense and transmit biometric signals.

Cover Film

A protective layer shields the ink and film from exposure.
Encapsulant

A thin, stretchable, and water-resistant layer.
Resistor

A thin layer of carbon or silver senses electrical currents and transmits data.
Conductor

A layer of silver transmits electrical currents throughout.
Base Film

A Thermoplastic Polyurethane (TPU) laminate stretches for seamless integration with textiles.
TEXTILE

Most preferred textiles can be used.

  • Soft

    With its smooth design, this form-fitting technology expands and flexes to optimize mobility and comfort.

  • Seamless

    Bonded directly to fabric, Intexar™ Health is thin and lightweight, making it easy to wear and easy to use.

  • Measured

    When placed directly on skin, Intexar™ Health has sensors that are able to detect the body’s natural electrical signals.

  • Therapeutic

    Gentle on-body heating and mild electro-stimulation are both possible, delivering pain relief to specific areas.

  • Assurance

    This technology is engineered to be a trusted partner for wearable health care.

  • Quality

    Backed by the reliability of DuPont, Intexar™ Health is designed to be dependable, washable, and functional.
