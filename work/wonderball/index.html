A Little Bite of History

Frankford has been in the candy business since 1947 but we still love a good surprise–that’s why we brought back Wonder Ball! You might remember when you opened the box, unwrapped the foil, and cracked open the chocolate ball to reveal a tasty candy tucked inside. Well, that feeling is back. If you’re new to Wonder Ball, there are a few things you might want to know. Every Wonder Ball comes with delicious chocolate, candy, and fun sticker surprises. The type of surprise you find depends on the kind of Wonder Ball you’re enjoying!

Original Wonder Ball: The classic you loved as a kid comes with a candy surprise and stickers.

Wonder Ball Plus Prize: All the great aspects of the original with an additional collectible prize.

Wonder Ball Minis: Perfect for sharing! 2 smaller sized Wonder Balls and stickers.
